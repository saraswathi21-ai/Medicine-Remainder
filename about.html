About Medicine Remainder

The Medicine Remainder is a simple yet powerful application designed to help users manage their medication schedules effectively. Our goal is to ensure that individuals never miss a dose, improving health outcomes and quality of life.

Key features include setting medicine names, dosage information, reminder timings, and even tracking missed doses. The app sends alerts to remind users at the right time and can notify family members if doses are repeatedly missed.

Whether you're managing a complex prescription schedule or helping a loved one with theirs, our user-friendly interface makes it easier to stay on track. We believe in the power of timely medication and hope to make your health journey smoother.
